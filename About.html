About Us

Welcome to Regreen, your comprehensive platform dedicated to fostering sustainable practices and addressing critical issues of food insecurity and plastic pollution in Lebanon. At Regreen, we believe in the power of collective action to create positive change and build a better future for our communities and our planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is twofold: to combat hunger by facilitating food donations to those in need and to mitigate plastic pollution by promoting recycling and responsible waste management practices. We strive to bridge the gap between surplus food resources and individuals facing food insecurity while simultaneously advocating for the reduction of single-use plastics and the promotion of eco-friendly alternatives.

What We Do

Through our website, individuals and organizations can easily locate donation centers and initiatives across Lebanon where they can contribute excess food items or plastic waste. We provide a user-friendly platform that connects donors with local charities, food banks, and recycling facilities, making it convenient for everyone to participate in the movement towards a more sustainable and equitable society.

At Regreen we are deeply committed to supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), align our efforts with the global agenda for sustainable development and contribute to creating a more inclusive and environmentally conscious world.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to make a meaningful difference in Lebanon's fight against food insecurity and plastic pollution. Whether you're an individual looking to donate food or plastic waste, or an organization interested in partnering with us to expand our impact, there are countless ways to get involved and support our cause.

Together, we can build a future where no one goes hungry, and our environment thrives free from the burden of plastic pollution. Thank you for being part of our journey towards a brighter, more sustainable tomorrow.
